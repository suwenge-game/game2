Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug or technical issue?
Please send an email to bugs@gamehub.com with a detailed description of the issue, including your browser type, device information, and steps to reproduce the problem.
Can I suggest new games for the platform?
Absolutely! We welcome game suggestions from our community. Send your recommendations to games@gamehub.com with details about the game and why you think it would be a good fit.
How long does it take to get a response?
We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. Business inquiries may take slightly longer due to the complexity of the request.
Do you offer business partnerships?
Yes, we're always interested in exploring partnerships with game developers, publishers, and other gaming-related businesses. Contact us at business@gamehub.com.
How can I provide feedback about the platform?
We value your feedback! You can send your suggestions and comments to feedback@gamehub.com or use the contact form above.
Is there a way to contact you for media inquiries?
For media and press inquiries, please contact us at press@gamehub.com. We'll get back to you as soon as possible.
